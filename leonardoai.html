Leonardo.ai

Leonardo.ai is a generative AI platform that creates high-quality images, art, and video from text prompts, custom models, and guided workflows.

Try Leonardo.ai ↗
Last updated 2026-04-01
Leonardo.ai is a powerful AI image and video generation platform best suited for marketing teams, product creators, and agencies who need consistent, professional-grade visuals with strong creative control. It offers guided workflows and superior quality on paid plans, but the credit-based system can become expensive for heavy users and the interface has a learning curve.
Strengths
  • Blueprint workflows provide guided, step-by-step creation for marketing assets and brand visuals
  • High image quality and fine-grained control compared to Midjourney or Firefly
  • Custom model training, API access, and enterprise scalability
Limitations
  • Credit-based pricing can add up quickly for frequent or advanced use
  • Frequent interface updates may disrupt established workflows
  • Free tier token limits restrict heavy daily usage

Overview 07

Leonardo.ai is a generative AI platform designed for creating high-quality images, art, and video from text prompts or custom models.

It offers guided Blueprint workflows for specific marketing and creative needs, custom model training, and advanced tools like video animation and image editing, all in one integrated platform.

Leonardo.ai is most valuable for product marketing teams, creative professionals, and businesses that need consistent, professional visuals without traditional production resources. The main tradeoff is the credit-based pricing and learning curve for advanced features.
