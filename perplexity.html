Overview 07

Perplexity Computer is a general-purpose AI agent that functions as a digital worker capable of operating computer interfaces and executing complex, multi-step workflows autonomously. Unlike traditional chatbots that provide text responses, Computer solves the problem of time-consuming manual tasks by creating and running entire workflows that can span hours or months, handling everything from web research and data extraction to app creation and tool integration.

The system works by accepting natural language prompts and deploying specialized subagents to handle different aspects of complex tasks. Computer can browse the web, automate browser actions, extract and synthesize data, connect to hundreds of tools including Gmail, Slack, Notion, and Calendar, and create production-ready deliverables like interactive reports, websites, and applications. Users can set recurring tasks for autonomous execution, iterate on ongoing work, or guide Computer through multi-step processes while it operates in the background.

Perplexity Computer is most valuable for professionals and businesses that need comprehensive research, analysis, and workflow automation capabilities, particularly those requiring cited, grounded information and interactive deliverables. However, the tool comes with significant tradeoffs: it's expensive at $200/month, limited to Pro and Max subscribers, and only available on web desktop and Slack. While users report impressive results with interactive dashboards and detailed reports that surpass text-based AI competitors, the high cost and platform limitations may restrict adoption to users with substantial automation needs and budgets.
