HeadshotPro is a budget-friendly AI headshot generator that delivers 40–240 professional-looking portraits from selfies for $29–$59. It’s a fast, affordable alternative to traditional photography, but many results don’t closely resemble the user and require heavy filtering to find usable shots.
Strengths
  • Costs $29–$59 versus $200+ for traditional professional headshots
  • Delivers 40–240 headshots in as little as 1–3 hours
  • Works with ordinary smartphone selfies
Limitations
  • Many generated images don’t accurately resemble the user
  • Requires 17+ high-quality input photos for best results
  • Some images show unrealistic body proportions or facial distortions

Overview 07

HeadshotPro is an AI headshot generator that creates professional business portraits from your selfies in minutes, replacing expensive traditional photography sessions.

Users upload 17+ photos, choose styles, and receive 40–240 AI-generated headshots within 1–3 hours. The service is popular for LinkedIn profiles, company websites, and personal branding because of its low cost and fast turnaround.

It works best for users who can provide good input photos and are willing to review many results. The main tradeoff is inconsistent facial resemblance — many images look similar but not exactly like the person.
