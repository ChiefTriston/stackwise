Overview 07

HeadshotPro is an AI-powered headshot generator that creates professional business portraits from user selfies, solving the problem of expensive and time-consuming traditional photography sessions. The service has created 18 million headshots for nearly 197,000 customers and promises professional results in minutes rather than scheduling formal photoshoots. Users can generate multiple professional headshots for platforms like LinkedIn and social media without the cost and logistics of hiring a professional photographer.

The tool works by having users upload 1-3 selfies, then selecting from available background and clothing style combinations. According to video evidence, users can choose from 24 different style options and receive up to 240 generated headshots within approximately 2 hours of processing. The AI creates variations by maintaining facial features while applying different professional backgrounds, lighting, and styling options. Users then review the generated options to select their preferred headshots for professional use.

HeadshotPro benefits professionals who need quick, affordable headshots for LinkedIn profiles, company websites, or social media, particularly those who want to avoid the expense and scheduling complexity of traditional photography. However, there are significant tradeoffs: customer reviews indicate only about 70% of generated images are usable quality, with some results not looking exactly like the person. Video evidence confirms that while the $49 price point offers good value for 240 options, users need to expect variability in results, with blurred backgrounds sometimes appearing obviously AI-generated compared to neutral backgrounds that look more natural.
