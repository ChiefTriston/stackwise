QuillBot

QuillBot is an AI-powered paraphrasing and writing assistant that helps users rewrite content, improve clarity, fix grammar, and avoid plagiarism across multiple languages and platforms.

Try QuillBot ↗
Last updated 2026-04-01
QuillBot is a strong AI writing assistant best suited for students, content creators, and professionals who need fast paraphrasing, grammar checking, and style enhancement. The Chrome extension and high-quality output make it very practical, but the free plan’s strict limits and occasional accuracy issues mean most serious users upgrade quickly.
Strengths
  • High-quality paraphrasing that produces fluent, natural-sounding text
  • Chrome extension integrates smoothly with Gmail, Google Docs, Slack, and other tools
  • Multiple modes and synonym slider give precise control over rewrite style
Limitations
  • Free plan limited to ~125 words per paraphrase, requiring constant workarounds
  • Occasional entity recognition issues and minor grammatical errors in output
  • Output can sometimes feel robotic and requires light human editing

Inside the workflow 05
Paste or type your text into the Paraphraser, adjust the synonym slider and choose a mode (Standard, Fluency, Creative, etc.), then copy the rewritten version. Most users run longer pieces through multiple short passes on the free plan and do light manual editing afterward.
  • —Free version’s 125-word limit forces breaking long content into small chunks
  • —Higher synonym settings can make text less accurate and more robotic
  • —Chrome extension sometimes has slight loading delays in Google Docs

Overview 07

QuillBot is an AI-powered writing assistant that solves the common problems of unclear, unprofessional, or repetitive writing by providing paraphrasing, grammar correction, and style enhancement tools. It helps users rewrite content quickly, improve clarity, and avoid plagiarism while maintaining the original meaning.

The platform offers a core paraphraser with multiple modes and an adjustable synonym slider, plus grammar checking, plagiarism detection, AI content detection, citation generation, and translation. It is accessible via web app or Chrome extension that works directly in Gmail, Google Docs, Slack, and other tools.

QuillBot is most valuable for students, content creators, copywriters, and non-native English speakers who need fast, reliable writing support. The main tradeoff is the heavily restricted free plan and the need for occasional human editing to ensure perfect accuracy.
