QuillBot is an AI-powered writing assistant that solves the common problems of unclear, unprofessional, or repetitive writing by providing paraphrasing, grammar correction, and style enhancement tools.

Try QuillBot ↗
Last updated 2026-04-01
QuillBot is a solid paraphrasing and writing enhancement tool that works well for students, content creators, and professionals who need to rewrite content while maintaining readability. The quality is high and it integrates well with popular platforms, but the free version has significant limitations that force frequent workarounds, and it struggles with entity recognition and can introduce grammatical errors.
Strengths
  • High-quality paraphrasing that maintains fluent, natural-sounding text
  • Chrome extension integrates seamlessly with Gmail, Google Docs, Slack and other platforms
  • Multiple paraphrasing modes with adjustable synonym slider for customized output
Limitations
  • Free version severely limited to 25 words, creating frequent copy-paste hassles
  • Poor entity recognition that doesn't understand context about people, places, or specific terms
  • Can introduce grammatical errors and inaccurate synonyms during paraphrasing

Inside the workflow 05
You paste or type text into QuillBot's paraphraser, adjust the synonym slider and select modes like Standard or Fluency based on your needs, then copy the rewritten output. You often need to run text through multiple times with different settings to get the right balance of readability and uniqueness, especially when working around the 125-word limit on the free plan.
  • —Free version's 125-word limit forces you to break longer content into small chunks and paraphrase piece by piece
  • —Increasing synonym usage can make text less accurate and harder to read, requiring manual cleanup
  • —Chrome extension takes time to load in Google Docs and other platforms, creating workflow delays during heavy usage

Overview 07

QuillBot is an AI-powered writing assistant that solves the common problems of unclear, unprofessional, or repetitive writing by providing paraphrasing, grammar correction, and style enhancement tools. The platform addresses time-consuming writing tasks, helps users avoid plagiarism through content rewriting, and assists non-native speakers in producing natural-sounding English text. As evidenced by long-term user testimonials spanning over two years of documented usage, QuillBot has established itself as a comprehensive writing solution that helps users craft polished, professional content efficiently.

The tool works through multiple integrated features including a core paraphrasing engine with various modes (Creative, Humanize, etc.), grammar and spell checking, plagiarism detection, AI content detection, and citation generation. Users can access QuillBot through web interfaces or browser extensions that integrate with platforms like Gmail, Google Docs, and Slack. The paraphrasing tool uses adjustable settings and synonym sliders to control the level of text transformation, allowing users to maintain original meaning while varying sentence structure and word choice. The platform offers both free and premium tiers, with premium modes providing more sophisticated rewriting options.

QuillBot benefits writers, copywriters, marketers, students, teachers, and non-native English speakers most significantly, with documented evidence showing particular value for content creators who write professionally and need daily writing assistance. However, key tradeoffs include substantial limitations in the free version that require workarounds, occasional issues with entity recognition and grammatical errors in output, and the need for human oversight to ensure quality. While the tool produces fluent, SEO-friendly content suitable for ranking purposes, users must balance the efficiency gains against potential accuracy concerns and the risk of over-relying on AI-generated content.
