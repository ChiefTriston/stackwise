GetResponse is an all-in-one email marketing and automation platform that helps businesses turn prospects into customers with AI-powered campaigns, landing pages, and lead generation tools.

Try GetResponse ↗
Last updated 2026-04-01
GetResponse is a solid all-in-one email marketing platform best suited for small to medium businesses that want easy automation, AI tools, and landing pages in one place. It offers good value with strong automation features, but some users report deliverability issues and occasional customer support delays.
Strengths
  • AI-powered tools for fast campaign and landing page creation
  • Visual automation builder with ready-made workflow templates
  • All-in-one platform with email, webinars, landing pages, and lead generation
Limitations
  • Some emails land in spam folders, requiring extra attention to deliverability
  • Short 14-day free trial limits full testing of advanced features
  • Not ideal for heavy e-commerce users according to some reviewers

Overview 07

GetResponse is an all-in-one email marketing and automation platform that helps businesses turn prospects into customers with AI-powered campaigns, landing pages, and lead generation tools.

It combines email marketing, visual automation builder, webinars, and landing pages in a single dashboard, making it easy for small to medium businesses to create and automate customer journeys.

GetResponse is most valuable for growing businesses that want an affordable, beginner-friendly platform with strong automation features. The main tradeoffs are occasional deliverability challenges and a short 14-day trial period.
