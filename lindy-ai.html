Overview 07

Lindy AI is a no-code platform that builds custom AI agents to automate business workflows, with the company promising to deploy production-ready agents within 48 hours of scoping your requirements. Unlike typical AI chatbots confined to browser interfaces, Lindy operates through text messaging (iMessage and SMS) and integrates with thousands of apps to handle tasks like email management, calendar coordination, lead research, and customer support responses.

The platform works by letting users either build their own agents through a no-code interface or have Lindy's team create custom agents after workflow consultation. These agents use large language models for contextual decision-making rather than simple if-then automation rules, with recent Claude Sonnet 4.5 integration improving coding-related tasks. Lindy agents can join email threads like human assistants, access your documentation and past support tickets to inform responses, and use "computer use" capabilities when standard API integrations aren't available. The system operates on a credit-based pricing model ($0.01-$0.10+ per task depending on complexity) with plans starting at $49/month for 5,000 credits.

This design benefits operations teams and business users who want sophisticated automation without coding expertise, particularly those handling repetitive customer communications or scheduling workflows. However, the credit system creates unpredictable costs that frustrate users, and Trustpilot shows a 2.4/5 rating with complaints about unauthorized charges and credits burning through quickly. Technical users who prefer transparent pricing or need detailed control over automation logic might find traditional workflow tools more suitable than Lindy's high-level "Chief of Staff" approach.
