InVideo

InVideo is an AI-powered video creation platform that turns text prompts, scripts, or ideas into full videos with stock footage, voiceovers, captions, and music — no editing experience required.

Try InVideo ↗
Last updated 2026-04-01
InVideo is a strong AI video generator best suited for beginners, content creators, and marketers who want to produce professional-looking videos from text prompts with minimal effort. It excels at faceless YouTube and social media content, but credit management and occasional repetitive visuals mean most users still do some manual editing.
Strengths
  • Text-to-video AI creates complete videos (script, visuals, voiceover, music) in minutes
  • Access to millions of premium stock clips from Shutterstock and iStock
  • Drag-and-drop editor is beginner-friendly with no prior video skills needed
Limitations
  • Credit-based system requires careful tracking and can get expensive at higher volumes
  • AI videos often feel repetitive and need manual tweaks for polish
  • Limited advanced editing tools compared to dedicated video editors

Overview 07

InVideo is an AI-powered video creation platform that turns text prompts, scripts, or ideas into full videos with stock footage, voiceovers, captions, and music — no editing experience required.

It combines a huge stock library, AI text-to-video generation, and an easy drag-and-drop editor so users can create promotional videos, explainers, faceless YouTube content, and social media clips in minutes.

InVideo is most valuable for content creators, marketers, and small businesses who need to produce video quickly and cost-effectively. The main tradeoff is the credit-based system and the fact that AI-generated videos almost always benefit from light human editing for the best results.
