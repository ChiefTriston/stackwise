Inside the workflow 05
You either write your own script or use InVideo's AI to generate one from prompts, then feed it into the AI text-to-video feature which automatically matches stock footage, adds captions and background music. You typically need to edit afterward, cleaning up AI-generated text that has 'wacky wording' and sometimes adjusting the automatically selected media since the AI can be repetitive with similar scripts.
  • —AI often reuses the same graphics and pictures for similar scripts, making videos feel repetitive if you create content in the same niche
  • —You need to carefully manage credit usage as the platform pushes upgrades, and free stock content results in noticeably lower video quality
  • —UI navigation is clunky - you can't preview images or videos in the media library without going back, and the script-to-video editor feels unintuitive

Overview 07

InVideo is an AI-powered video creation platform that transforms text prompts, scripts, and ideas into professional-quality videos without requiring technical video editing skills. The platform addresses the significant time, cost, and expertise barriers that prevent individuals and businesses from creating engaging video content for marketing, education, and social media purposes. By leveraging AI automation and providing access to over 200 AI models including advanced video generation tools like Veo 3.1, Sora 2 Pro, and Kling 3.0, InVideo democratizes video production and enables users to create content that would traditionally require professional video editing software and extensive technical knowledge.

The platform operates through multiple approaches: users can input text prompts to generate complete videos using the InVideo v4 agent (capable of creating up to 30-minute videos from single prompts), convert existing scripts into faceless YouTube videos through drag-and-drop editing, or utilize AI-generated scripts from simple prompts. The system integrates comprehensive stock media libraries from providers like iStock and Storyblocks, offers AI avatar creation for user-generated content (UGC) ads, and includes advanced features like inpainting and cleanup tools. Users can create various video types including promotional ads, explainer videos, product showcases, and social media content through an intuitive browser-based interface that works across Windows, MacOS, Chrome OS, and Linux.

InVideo primarily benefits content creators, marketers, small business owners, and social media managers who need to produce high-quality videos quickly and cost-effectively without extensive video editing expertise. The platform is particularly valuable for creating faceless YouTube videos, product advertisements, and branded content at scale. However, users must carefully manage credit usage as the AI model access operates on a consumption basis with variable pricing, and while the AI generates solid baseline content, some videos may require additional manual editing to achieve optimal quality and customization for specific brand requirements.
