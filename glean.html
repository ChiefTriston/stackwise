Overview 07

Glean is a company-wide AI platform that solves the universal workplace problem of finding information and people within enterprise organizations. Unlike in-app assistants that work within specific tools, Glean connects to all enterprise data sources to build comprehensive company context, helping knowledge workers locate relevant information, understand complex topics, and get trustworthy answers across their entire organizational ecosystem. The platform addresses the productivity drain caused by information silos and scattered knowledge across multiple enterprise systems.

Glean works by integrating with 100+ enterprise systems through native, permission-aware connectors that maintain existing access controls end-to-end. The platform initially connects to systems in read-only mode to build trust, then adds guardrails and automation capabilities once established. It functions as a conversational AI assistant similar to ChatGPT but with full company context, reading documents, conversations, and meetings across all connected systems. Users interact through a unified search and chat interface, receiving source-backed AI-generated answers without switching between different applications. The system also supports workflow automation through Glean Agents and a library of actions for cross-system operations.

Glean benefits large enterprises most, with deployments at companies like T-Mobile, Uber, and Dell that have complex legacy environments and conservative risk profiles. Organizations with diverse tech stacks spanning Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Slack, Confluence, Salesforce, and other enterprise tools see the greatest value from Glean's ecosystem-agnostic approach. The key tradeoff is that while Glean excels at cross-system integration and company-wide context, organizations heavily invested in a single ecosystem (like pure Microsoft 365 shops) might find ecosystem-specific solutions like Microsoft Copilot more tightly integrated for in-app productivity tasks, though they would sacrifice the broader organizational knowledge discovery that Glean provides.
