Overview 07

Glean is a company-wide AI platform that serves as an enterprise search and knowledge management solution, designed to connect and analyze data across all of an organization's systems rather than being confined to a single application suite. The platform addresses the critical enterprise problem of information silos and knowledge fragmentation by building comprehensive context across every system, learning from content, activity, and user behavior to deliver relevant, trustworthy answers and automate workflows. Unlike application-specific AI assistants, Glean positions itself as ecosystem-agnostic, helping employees find and understand information across their entire company rather than just creating and processing information within specific apps.

Glean operates through 100+ turnkey, permission-aware integrations that maintain access controls end-to-end while connecting to enterprise tools including Google Drive, Slack, Confluence, Jira, Zendesk, Salesforce, Microsoft 365, GitHub, and custom data sources. The platform brings AI-powered search and chat capabilities directly into workflows, such as within Microsoft Teams, allowing employees to ask questions and receive source-backed, AI-generated answers without switching between applications. Additionally, Glean offers automation capabilities through Glean Agents and a library of 100+ actions, including MCP host actions, enabling teams to run permission-aware automations that can retrieve information, reason over data, and execute workflows across multiple systems securely and at scale.

Glean benefits organizations most when they operate across multiple data sources and platforms, particularly those not exclusively using Microsoft's ecosystem, as it excels at multi-source integration compared to tools like Microsoft Copilot which perform best within Microsoft-owned products. The platform is especially valuable for enterprises struggling with information discovery across diverse tech stacks and those needing to maintain strict permission controls while enabling cross-system automation. However, organizations heavily invested in a single ecosystem might find more focused solutions sufficient, and the comprehensive nature of Glean's platform may represent overkill for companies with simpler, more centralized data architectures or those primarily seeking in-app productivity enhancements rather than enterprise-wide knowledge management.
