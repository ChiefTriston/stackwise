Synthesia

Synthesia is an AI video creation platform that turns text scripts into professional videos using realistic AI avatars and voice synthesis — no cameras, actors, or editing skills required.

Try Synthesia ↗
Last updated 2026-04-01
Synthesia is a premium AI video generator best suited for businesses and teams that need scalable, professional-looking training, marketing, or explainer videos. It delivers excellent avatar realism and voice cloning, but the 24-hour custom avatar processing time and high pricing make it less ideal for quick or low-budget projects.
Strengths
  • Highly realistic avatars with excellent lip-sync and voice cloning
  • Smart AI that suggests relevant stock footage based on your script
  • Supports 160+ languages and easy bulk video creation
Limitations
  • Custom avatars take up to 24 hours to process
  • Very limited free tier and relatively expensive paid plans
  • Generation can take several minutes per video

Overview 07

Synthesia is an AI video creation platform that turns text scripts into professional videos using realistic AI avatars and voice synthesis — no cameras, actors, or editing skills required.

Users can create custom avatars from a short webcam recording or use pre-built ones that speak in 160+ languages. The platform supports bulk video creation, spreadsheet personalization, and easy integration with learning management systems, making it popular for training, marketing, and internal communications.

Synthesia is most valuable for companies that regularly produce video content and can afford the premium pricing. The main tradeoffs are the 24-hour custom avatar processing time and higher cost compared to simpler tools.
